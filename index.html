About


Ellie Wem is a student at Makers Academy learning computer programming. Previously, she worked in law for three years after completing her degree at the University of East Anglia in 2015. The skills that she acquired during her professional life transposed themselves into a keen interest in programming and talking about herself in the third person. Her main interests include minimalist front-end design; creating workable, adaptable solutions; and collaborating with others as a way of approaching challenges and achieving the best results possible.

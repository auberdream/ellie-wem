About


Ellie Wem is a student at Makers Academy learning computer programming. Previously, she worked in law for three years after completing her law degree at the University of East Anglia in 2015. The skills that she acquired during this period transposed themselves into a keen interest in programming and talking about herself in the third person. Her main interests include minimalist front-end design; readable, concise code; and open communication.
